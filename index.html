Cats!

I love dogs, but I'm 100% a cat person. I volunteered at a local cat shelter in high school, and it was an amazing experience. I had the opportunity to meet new kittens every week, and I got to learn a lot about their behavior and just how fun and unique they can be. I've attatched pictures to share the cuteness of some of the cats I spent time with!

Tennis!

Tennis is another hobby of mine, and I've been playing tennis for nearly 10 years now. It's definitely my favorite sport to both play and watch (it's also the only sport I watch haha). I started out playing for fun with my dad and my sister, and eventually I ended up on my highschool's team, where I was even team captain for a year. It's a great way to destress, and I've met so many cool people through it!

Books!

“A room without books is like a body without a soul.” ~G.K. Chesterton. This was the first thing I added to the website's background, since you obviously can't have a shelf without books. I've always loved reading, it's one of my favorite hobbies, and I doubt I'll ever get bored of it. I've read books from almost all genres, but I've listed some of my favorites here for you to check out!

Art!

I love to draw, as you could probably already tell from the website interface! I've tried a variety of styles, from acrylics to oil paints, pastels, colored pencils, and even graphic design. I don't really have a preferred art style, I just like finding new ways to express myself. One of the best parts of creating this website was starting from scratch and being able to customize it, down to every last detail. Even though it may not look like a typical, generic website, I think it does a pretty good job of encompassing who I am through a screen!
