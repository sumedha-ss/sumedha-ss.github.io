Cats!

I love dogs, but I'm 100% a cat person. I volunteered at a local cat shelter for a year in high school, and it was an amazing experience. Not only did I have the opportunity to meet new kittens every week, I also got to learn a lot about their behavior and just how unique they can be. It was a super fulfilling experience that further strengthened my love for these adorable animals.
